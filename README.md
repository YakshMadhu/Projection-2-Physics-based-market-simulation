# Projection-2-Physics-based-market-simulation

**Can stock-market-like behavior emerge from physics-based particle interactions instead of traditional economic rules?, This is the essential question**
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Before I start programming, what does app actually do? If I could answer this question within few sentences then I have successfully paved the path for future steps

**Answer: A thought-experiment stock market simulator where price movement emerges from physics particle interactions instead of normal financial formulas. It's a market behaviour --> instead of a graph is shown as a live particle system of molecules --> user can control the forces to influence the behaviour --> emergent output through particle interaction + 3D heat map.**

But why make something like this? what will its usefullness be? what makes it uniuqe?

**Answer: I am making this program because I trade on the side and often hear market terms like liquidity, demand, supply, fear, momentum, and volume. This project is my way of experimenting to see whether stock market behaviour must be modeled only through economic rules, or whether it can also be recreated using physics-based particle, basically where or not the behviour of stock market can also emerge from another field set of rules, in this case physics. It's uniqueness comes from trating the stock market as experimental physics system rather than a traditional fiannce model.**
